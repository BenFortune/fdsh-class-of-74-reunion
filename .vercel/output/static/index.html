Fort Dodge Senior High Class of '74

50th Class Reunion August 9-10, 2024

Friday Night at Rides Bar & Grill

Saturday at the Cardiff Event Center 5pm - 11pm

Let's Celebrate!

Believe it or not 2024 will mark the 50th year since we graduated from Fort Dodge Senior High. The Class of 74 Reunion Planning Committee would appreciate your help/attendance in celebrating this milestone. Part of the planning is knowing how many classmates will be able to attend. Please let one of the Committee Member classmates noted below, if you can, or cannot attend.

The Reunion is still in the planning stage. However, the following activities have been confirmed:
  • Meet and greet get together will be held on Friday August 9 at Rides Bar and Grille. Snacks will be provided. Adult beverages will be available at an additional cost.
  • A sit-down meal will be held on Saturday Evening August 10 at The Cardiff Center. The Brookstone Inn & Suites (built in 2019) parking lot is connected to the Cardiff Center.
Other activities include:

  • The recently Renovated Lamar Ballroom
  • Feeling like a teenager again by walking the halls of Fort Dodge Senior High

Stay tuned to either the FDHS Class of 74 Facebook page or this website page for updates and costs. We look forward to a great 50th reunion filled with many conversations and laughs!
